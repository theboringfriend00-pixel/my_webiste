What we handle

Everything HR, minus the headcount.

Most SMEs don't need a full HR department — they need the right pieces, done properly. Pick what your business needs today, and add more as you grow.

01

Contracts & Compliance

Employment contracts, staff handbooks and policies drafted to meet the Employment Act, 2007 and current labour regulations.

02

Recruitment & Onboarding

Job descriptions, shortlisting, interviews and a smooth first 90 days — so new hires stick around and start contributing fast.

03

Payroll Oversight

Payroll accuracy checks, statutory deductions (NSSF, SHIF, NITA, PAYE) and payslip structuring in partnership with your accountant.

04

Performance & Culture

Simple, usable performance review systems and culture-building practices sized for teams of 5 to 100 people.

05

Employee Relations

Disciplinary processes, grievance handling and exits managed correctly — reducing legal exposure and protecting your reputation.

06

HR Strategy & Structure

Org design, role clarity and pay structures that scale cleanly as you move from 10 to 50 employees.
